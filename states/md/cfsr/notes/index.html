CFSR Notes & Documentation

Technical notes, methodology updates, and important information about CFSR Data Profile reports.

October 2025

Data Source Updates

Beginning with the August 2025 report, maltreatment recurrence calculations now include a 6-month lookback period to align with federal CFSR standards.

May 2025

Reporting Period Changes

CFSR Data Profile reports are now generated semi-annually (February and August) instead of quarterly to align with state fiscal year reporting cycles.

January 2025

New Performance Indicators

Added three new well-being outcome measures related to educational stability and mental health service delivery for children in foster care.
